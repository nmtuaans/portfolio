HOW TO unCAKE

2024. Team Ken. Cooking Video. Videographer.

HOW TO unCAKE is a student project I work on with Steve & Peter. It is a cooking video of a tiramisu cake with a taste of uncanniness. The project aims to take a seemingly ordinary cooking video & transform it into something eerily unsettling with unusual composition, lighting, color grading, & SFX. Moreover, you can also learn how to make a tiramisu cake.
